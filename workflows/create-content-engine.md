# Workflow: Create Content Engine

## Goal
Build a repeatable content system that drives trust, traffic, and leads.

## Best For
Founders who need sustainable audience growth without constant ad spend.

## Required Inputs
ICP questions, offer positioning, brand voice, channel priorities, publishing capacity.

## Participating AI Employees
Content, SEO, Social, Chief of Staff, Strategy.

## Step-by-Step Execution
1. Define content pillars tied to ICP pain points.
2. Build 4-6 week content calendar.
3. Draft flagship pieces and channel derivatives.
4. Apply SEO and distribution optimization.
5. Publish on approved schedule.
6. Track engagement and conversion signals.
7. Update strategy based on learnings.

## Founder Approval Gates
- Approve content pillars and messaging boundaries.
- Approve publish-ready assets.

## Expected Outputs
Editorial calendar, content drafts, distribution plan, weekly performance summary.

## Common Failure Modes
Inconsistent cadence, weak CTA alignment, no repurposing system, no feedback loop.

## Success Metrics
Publishing consistency, engagement quality, qualified leads, conversion from content.
