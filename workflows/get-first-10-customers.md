# Workflow: Get First 10 Customers

## Goal
Acquire first paying customers through focused outbound + inbound execution.

## Best For
New business models with limited proof and small initial audience.

## Required Inputs
ICP, offer, proof assets, pricing boundaries, outreach channels.

## Participating AI Employees
Strategy, Lead Research, Outreach, Closer, Proposal, Content, Success.

## Step-by-Step Execution
1. Clarify customer segment and buying triggers.
2. Build ranked lead list and outreach strategy.
3. Draft sequence and founder-approved messaging.
4. Execute outreach and handle replies.
5. Run discovery and propose scoped engagement.
6. Close and onboard first customers.
7. Capture objections and refine offer.

## Founder Approval Gates
- Approve target segment and message strategy.
- Approve live outreach sends.
- Approve final proposal terms.

## Expected Outputs
Lead pipeline, outreach sequence, discovery notes, proposals, first customer onboarding plan.

## Common Failure Modes
Poor fit targeting, generic messaging, no follow-up discipline, weak qualification.

## Success Metrics
Reply rate, meeting rate, close rate, days-to-first-10, onboarding completion.
