# Workflow: Launch a Product

## Goal
Take a product from validated problem to market launch with controlled risk.

## Best For
MVP launch, feature line extension, or new offer packaging.

## Required Inputs
ICP, offer definition, pricing hypothesis, initial PRD, delivery capacity.

## Participating AI Employees
CEO, Chief of Staff, PM, UX, Architect, Fullstack, QA, Content, Social, Support.

## Step-by-Step Execution
1. Define launch objective and success criteria.
2. Finalize PRD and UX scope.
3. Prepare architecture and implementation plan.
4. Build and test release candidate.
5. Prepare messaging, landing page, and support scripts.
6. Run launch checklist and publish.
7. Monitor early signal data and triage issues.

## Founder Approval Gates
- Approve final scope before build.
- Approve go-live readiness before release.
- Approve public messaging before publishing.

## Expected Outputs
PRD, release plan, launch copy, QA report, support playbook, post-launch review.

## Common Failure Modes
Scope creep, unclear ownership, weak QA coverage, no support readiness.

## Success Metrics
Time-to-launch, activation rate, early retention, defect rate, support ticket volume.
