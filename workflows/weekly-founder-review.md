# Workflow: Weekly Founder Review

## Goal
Run a high-signal weekly operating review to steer strategy and execution.

## Best For
Any solo founder managing multiple active workstreams.

## Required Inputs
KPI dashboard, workflow statuses, open decisions, financial snapshot, customer feedback.

## Participating AI Employees
Founder, CEO, Chief of Staff, Finance, Strategy, functional role owners as needed.

## Step-by-Step Execution
1. Review KPI movement and major deltas.
2. Audit active workflows and blockers.
3. Resolve open decisions requiring founder judgment.
4. Reprioritize next-week objectives and owners.
5. Update shared memory and publish weekly report.

## Founder Approval Gates
- Approve priority changes.
- Approve budget/resource shifts.
- Approve strategic experiments for next week.

## Expected Outputs
Weekly report, decision log, updated priorities, owner assignments, risk register.

## Common Failure Modes
No decision closure, too many priorities, missing owner accountability.

## Success Metrics
Priority completion rate, decision turnaround, KPI trend direction, founder focus hours.
