# Example: Creator Business (Niche Education Brand)

## Scenario
A solo creator monetizes expertise through paid guides and a cohort-based workshop.

## Starting Point
- Audience channel: LinkedIn + newsletter
- Current pain: inconsistent publishing and weak conversion path
- Founder goal: 500 new subscribers + 20 paid workshop seats in 60 days

## Workflow Stack
1. `workflows/create-content-engine.md`
2. `workflows/get-first-10-customers.md` (for offer conversion)
3. `workflows/weekly-founder-review.md`

## 60-Day Execution Plan
- **Weeks 1-2:** define content pillars, lead magnet, and landing-page conversion path.
- **Weeks 3-4:** publish weekly flagship content + daily derivatives.
- **Weeks 5-6:** run founder-approved outreach to warm leads and past customers.
- **Weeks 7-8:** launch workshop offer and execute onboarding/support loop.

## Expected Artifacts
- 6-week content calendar
- Lead magnet and landing page copy
- Offer announcement sequence
- Weekly growth dashboard
