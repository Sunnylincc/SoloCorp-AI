# Ideal Customer Profile

## Primary Segment
- Small B2B teams and solo operators buying outcome-driven services or products.

## Buying Triggers
- Need faster execution with limited headcount.
- Existing process is inconsistent or hard to scale.
- Revenue impact tied to speed and operational quality.

## Qualifiers
- Has budget for implementation.
- Can provide required data and approvals.
- Values process transparency and measurable results.

## Disqualifiers
- No decision authority.
- Wants undefined “AI magic” without operational input.
- Misaligned timeline or compliance constraints.
