# Offers

## Core Offer 1: Build
Design and launch a focused product or service with clear positioning and execution workflows.

## Core Offer 2: Grow
Install demand generation systems (content, SEO, outreach, sales workflows).

## Core Offer 3: Operate
Run ongoing optimization cycles: product improvements, customer success, and performance reporting.

## Packaging Rules
- Define scope, timeline, owner, and acceptance criteria.
- Tie each offer to measurable business outcomes.
- Keep optional add-ons explicit and limited.
