# Pricing

## Model
Use value-linked pricing with clear delivery tiers.

## Example Tier Logic
- Starter: fixed scope, founder-led approvals, weekly reporting.
- Growth: broader scope, cross-functional workflows, twice-weekly reporting.
- Scale: multi-workstream execution, custom KPIs, dedicated review cadence.

## Pricing Guardrails
- Never quote without scope assumptions.
- Include change-order policy.
- Clarify what is excluded.
