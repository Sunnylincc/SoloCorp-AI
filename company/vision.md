# Vision

Build a durable one-person company that delivers premium outcomes through a coordinated team of AI employees, with the founder steering strategy and approvals.

## 3-Year Direction
- Operate multiple revenue streams with shared infrastructure.
- Standardize execution into reusable workflows.
- Maintain high trust through quality, consistency, and clear communication.

## Strategic Principles
1. Solve expensive, recurring customer problems.
2. Prefer systems over one-off heroics.
3. Document every decision that should scale.
4. Keep founder time focused on leverage.
