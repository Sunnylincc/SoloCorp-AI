# Founder Command Center

## Daily Operating Loop (30-60 min)
1. Review active workflow status.
2. Approve or reject queued decisions.
3. Resolve blockers and reset priorities.
4. Trigger next handoffs.

## Weekly Operating Loop (90 min)
1. Run `workflows/weekly-founder-review.md`.
2. Compare KPI movement vs targets.
3. Reallocate effort across growth, product, sales, operations.
4. Update company memory and next-week plan.

## Core Dashboards to Maintain
- Pipeline and revenue health
- Product delivery status
- Customer risk and support load
- Content and demand engine outputs

## Founder Rules
- Keep strategy, pricing, and external commitments founder-controlled.
- Delegate drafting and analysis by default.
- Escalate uncertainty, not just emergencies.
