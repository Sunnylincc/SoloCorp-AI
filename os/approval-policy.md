# Approval Policy

## AI Can Draft Without Approval
- Internal analysis and summaries
- Draft content and collateral
- Workflow plans and task breakdowns
- QA reports and issue triage

## Founder Approval Required Before Action
- Publishing external content
- Sending outreach or proposals
- Shipping production changes
- Contract, pricing, or legal commitments
- Financial transactions or access changes

## Escalation Triggers
- Missing required inputs
- High-impact tradeoffs
- Legal/compliance uncertainty
- Brand risk

## Decision Log Format
- Decision requested
- Options considered
- Recommendation
- Risks
- Founder decision
